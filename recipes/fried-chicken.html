Fried Chicken

Fried chicken is a staple of the south east United States. It is first hand breaded with flour, egg, and bread crumbs. Typically, the breast, leg's, thigh's and wing's are fried.

Fried chicken can be made into many types of dishes and is also common among many different culture. On occasion fried chicken is made without breading, but usually that is not the case. It is more health, but it sacrifices flavor.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • flour
  • bread crumbs
  • chicken pieces
  • oil

Instructions

  1. bread chicken
  2. head oil
  3. fry chicken
  4. remove from oil
  5. remove from pan and place onto paper towel
  6. allow oil to settle for the chicken for 2 minutes
  7. enjoy!
