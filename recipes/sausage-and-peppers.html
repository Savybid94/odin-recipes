Fried Sausage and Peppers

This dish is a classic Italian dish as well. With a wide variety of ingredients that can be substituted it is perfect to make once a week. It is healthy and carbohydrate free. Feel free to add some if you want, rice is suggested. You can substituted almost any ingredients you want to make it your own. Try chicken tenderloin for a meat substitute, maybe squash for the peppers.

It is a common dish in many cultures and each do it a bit differently so experiment with what you like best! Remember with cooking its all about your preference and the preferences of the people you are feeding.

Ingredients

  • 2 whole Peppers
  • 1 whole sweet onion
  • dice or slice onion and peppers
  • 1 pack of spicey Sausage
  • ground fennel seeds
  • ground red pepper
  • olive oil

Instructions

  1. heat oil
  2. add ground red pepper and fennel
  3. add cut onion and peppers
  4. fry and stir occasionally until desired texture is achieved
  5. grill sausage on a griddle until fully cooked
  6. place grilled sausage on top of the onion and peppers
  7. Enjoy!
