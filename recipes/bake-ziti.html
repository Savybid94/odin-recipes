Bake Ziti an Italian Dish

Baked Ziti is a classic Italian dish. The dish, as is with many Italian dishes is a mixture of tomato sauce, cheese and pasta. For this recipe you need fresh saws, canned will not do. You need cheese, and multiple kinds, mozzarella and ricotta. The pasta for this this is ziti as the name implies.

The alfredo sauce can be substituted for the tomato sauce. As well, many types of pasta can take the place of ziti, most pastes can excluding pastas similar to spaghetti, fettucini, linguine.

Ingredients

  • pasta of choice
  • canned tomato's
  • garlic
  • oil
  • ground red pepper
  • mozzarella
  • ricotta

Instructions

  1. Start boiling 4 qts of water
  2. Heat oil
  3. When hot add garlic and red pepper let brown
  4. Add tomato puree
  5. Fry for 20 to 25 minutes while stirring occasionally
  6. Add pasta to water and boil for 12-15 minutes depending on texture preference
  7. Strain and let sit for 2 minutes
  8. Mix sauce, pasta, and ricotta together
  9. Sprinkle mozzarella on top
  10. Bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes
